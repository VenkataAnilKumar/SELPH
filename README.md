## What is SELPH?

SELPH is a **universal Digital Twin AI** that learns your communication style, voice, avatar, expertise, and topic boundaries вҖ” then drafts responses on your behalf across any channel, with **you always in the loop**.

It is not a chatbot. It is not an assistant. It **is you** вҖ” available 24/7.

```
Fan sends a DM at 3 AM
        вҶ“
SELPH loads your identity model (voice, style, topics, briefings, sender tier)
        вҶ“
Drafts a reply that sounds exactly like you вҖ” warm, casual, on-brand
        вҶ“
Sends you a push notification: "Draft ready. 92% confidence."
        вҶ“
You tap Approve in 2 seconds. Done.
        вҶ“
Your fan thinks you wrote it. You kind of did вҖ” your twin did.
        вҶ“
SELPH logs the approval. Updates your model. Gets better.
```

## What's Been Built

> This is a working product вҖ” not a prototype. Every feature below is implemented, tested, and shipped.

**Identity & Onboarding**
- Twin profile with domain, tone, vocabulary, and communication style
- Topic expertise model вҖ” twin knows what you know and how confident you are
- Identity verification and consent architecture per channel and feature
- Onboarding status tracking and iterative profile refinement

**Twin Engine**
- Context loading with semantic search across your identity profile
- Channel-aware prompt building (Instagram vs Gmail behave differently)
- Confidence scoring on every draft вҖ” low confidence always escalates to you
- Content moderation on all outputs, always on

**Human-in-the-Loop Approval**
- Approve, edit, or reject any draft in one tap
- Rejection reasons feed back into the twin вҖ” it learns what you don't sound like
- Push notifications with draft previews
- Emergency pause from the home screen

**Voice & Avatar**
- Voice clone using Chatterbox (MIT, free by default) or ElevenLabs (paid optional)
- Avatar generation using open-source providers (free default) or HeyGen (paid optional)
- Consent and enrollment flow for both; assets stored in encrypted cloud storage

**Advanced Features**
- **Twin Briefing** вҖ” inject real-time context into every draft (conference week, availability, opinions)
- **VIP Sender Tiers** вҖ” route your most important people directly to you, bypassing the twin
- **Batch Pattern Approval** вҖ” cluster similar messages, approve 50 personalized replies in one tap
- **Batch Personalization** вҖ” each approved batch reply is uniquely tailored per sender

**Channels (Live)**
- Instagram DMs вҖ” OAuth + webhook pipeline
- Gmail вҖ” OAuth + push subscription pipeline

**Analytics & Quality**
- Approval rate tracking (your north star metric)
- Weekly digest вҖ” messages handled, time saved, top topics
- Performance summary and quality trend over time
- Creator referral program

## Three Features No One Else Has

### 1. Twin Briefing вҖ” Real-Time Context Injection

Tell your twin something that matters right now. Every draft reflects it until it expires.

```json
POST /v1/identity/briefings

  "type": "availability",
  "content": "I'm at a conference this week. Keep replies brief and offer a call next week.",
  "expires_at": "2026-05-05T00:00:00Z"

```

Briefing types: `fact` В· `opinion` В· `instruction` В· `availability` В· `boundary`

No prompt engineering. No remembering to add context. Your twin just knows.

### 2. VIP Sender Tiers вҖ” Your Twin Knows Who Matters

```
Tier 0  VIP      вҶ’  Routes straight to you. Twin never sees it.
Tier 1  Priority вҶ’  Twin drafts, but you always review вҖ” no auto-send ever.
Tier 2  Standard вҶ’  Normal twin flow. Approve in one tap.
Tier 3  Cold     вҶ’  Twin filters and handles; minimal attention needed.
```

Your closest relationships bypass the twin entirely. The twin suggests tier upgrades as trust builds.

## Safety & Ethics

SELPH is built trust-first, not growth-first.

- **Always transparent** вҖ” recipients can verify any twin interaction via cryptographic link
- **C2PA watermarking** on all twin-generated content вҖ” traceable forever
- **Hard limits by default** вҖ” financial, legal, medical actions always blocked
- **Anomaly detection** вҖ” auto-pause if twin behavior deviates from baseline
- **Full audit log** вҖ” every twin action logged immutably
- **Consent architecture** вҖ” explicit consent required per channel, per feature
- **Compliant from day 1** вҖ” GDPR, CCPA, EU AI Act, India IT Rules
- **VIP bypass** вҖ” your most important relationships never touch the twin

Policy decisions: [SELPH_Canonical-Policy-Matrix.md](docs/04-safety/SELPH_Canonical-Policy-Matrix.md)
