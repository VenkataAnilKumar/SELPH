## What SELPH Is

SELPH is a universal digital twin platform that learns a person’s communication style, expertise, and preferences, then drafts responses on their behalf across channels with human approval in the loop.

Unlike generic assistants, SELPH is identity-first. It is designed to represent how a user actually communicates, not just complete tasks.

## Problem

High-agency professionals and creators face interaction overload:
- Creators cannot respond at scale while staying authentic.
- Consultants and freelancers lose opportunities due to delayed replies.
- Developers and operators spend excessive time in repetitive communication loops.
- Executives face decision and messaging bottlenecks.

Current tools solve fragments such as drafting, voice, or avatars. They do not deliver a unified identity twin with trust controls.

## Solution

SELPH combines:
1. Identity model
- Voice, style, domain expertise, topic boundaries, and channel behavior.

2. Twin engine
- Context loading, prompt generation, confidence scoring, and moderation.

3. Human-in-the-loop control
- Approve, edit, reject, or skip every draft.

4. Safety by design
- Consent architecture, audit trail, abuse reporting, and anomaly detection.
